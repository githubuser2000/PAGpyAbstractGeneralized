# Currency by Scale

*Note: This table describes the model as an abstract semantic structure, not as a legal or ethical judgment.*

| Currency                                                                                       | Scale                    | Calculation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Number**                                                                                     | Country                  | For each country, the currency is a **number**.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| **Angle**                                                                                      | Several allied countries | The currency is an **angle** with two axes: **good vs. evil**, fuzzily defined by several governments, and **popular vs. unpopular**, determined by the population. The number of the good or popular corresponds to the **semicircle circumference**. Through **tan** or **cotan**, the value range is mapped from **minus infinity to plus infinity**. This currency is plus/minus invertible through the **galaxy currency** and concatenable through the **galaxy-cluster currency**. |
| **Space instead of number**                                                                    | Defense organization     | For each defense organization, the currency is **space instead of number**. This space can have **arbitrarily many dimensions** and can be converted into a number through **volume**. The currency means **possession rather than ownership**, including possession of people within the model.                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| **Currency as the unit for assets**                                                            | UN assets                | For stocks, derivatives, funds, certificates, and the foreign exchange market, the unit is the respective **currency**.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Stacked fuzzy truth values**                                                                 | Planet                   | Truth values from **−1 = lie** to **+1 = truth** are stacked and thereby converted into a **number**.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **Hierarchy**                                                                                  | Star system              | Hierarchy is made convertible into a number through **2 sums** and **multiplication**.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **Topological monoid for unary operations**                                                    | Galaxy                   | Unary operations allow inversion or twisting: numbers positive/negative, hierarchy parts of products or humans, truth ↔ lie.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **Topological group of addition-like calculations**                                            | Cluster of galaxies      | Addition-like operations are applied to smaller currencies, for example to concatenate hierarchy or to combine lower-order scales.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Stacked for-loop circulations with circular-economy debt and circular-economy contribution** | Universe                 | Parts of a for-loop are stacked: circular-economy debt, for example compost, plus circular-economy contribution. One unit arises from a point of the for-loop plus circulations or repetitions up to that point. With **20 repetitions per loop**, **3 loop runs = 60 currency units**.                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
